PRAVESHA PIXEL FACEFEAST 2K26
Event Description
The Face Painting event (Tech Theme) offers a creative platform for participants to visually represent technological concepts through artistic expression. Participants will engage in a structured activity involving a painting phase followed by a brief showcase and explanation of their design.

Rules & Regulations
The event is open to teams of 2 members (artist and model).
Each team will be given 60 minutes to create your model.
Only face painting is permitted; the use of external props is not allowed.
Participants must bring their own materials.
The design must clearly reflect a Computer Science or technology-based theme (e.g., AI, circuits, robotics).
Participants must complete and present their work within the allotted time.
All participants must adhere to the instructions given by the event coordinators.
Judges’ decision will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria
Creativity and Originality - Evaluates the uniqueness of the vision and whether the entry offers a fresh perspective that avoids clichés.
Theme Relevance - Measures how effectively the work connects to the specific topic and fulfills the core requirements of the prompt.
Detailing and Execution - Assesses the technical quality, craftsmanship, and the level of care taken in the final finish of the project.
Innovation in Concept -Focuses on the "big idea" and whether the approach uses clever, forward-thinking solutions to solve a problem or express a thought.
Presentation and Explanation -Examines the clarity of the delivery and how well the participant communicates their process and reasoning.
